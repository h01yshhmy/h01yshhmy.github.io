P3911 最小公倍数之和

记 \(C_x\) 为 \(\sum_{i=1}^n[A_i=x]\)，\(L\) 为 \(\max_{i=1}^n A_i\)。 \[ \begin{aligned} &\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\textrm{lcm}(A_i,A_j)\\ =&\sum_{i=1}^L\sum_{j=1}^LC_iC_j\times \textrm{lcm}(i,j)\\ =&\sum_{i=1}^L\sum_{j=1}^L\frac{iC_i\times jC_j}{\gcd(i,j)}\\ =&\sum_{i=1}^L\sum_{j=1}^L\sum_{k=1}^L\frac{iC_i\times jC_j}{k}\times [\gcd(i,j)=k]\\ =&\sum_{k=1}^Lk^2\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{k}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{k}\rfloor}\frac{iC_{ik}\times jC_{jk}}{k}\times [\gcd(i,j)=1]\\ =&\sum_{k=1}^L\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{k}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{k}\rfloor}\sum_{d|\gcd(i,j)}\mu(d)\times iC_{ik}\times jC_{jk}\times k\\ =&\sum_{k=1}^Lk\sum_{d=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{k}\rfloor}\mu(d)\times d^2\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{kd}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{kd}\rfloor} iC_{ikd}\times jC_{jkd}\\ =&\sum_{k=1}^Lkd\sum_{kd\le L}\mu(d)\times d\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{kd}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{kd}\rfloor} iC_{ikd}\times jC_{jkd}\\ =&\sum_{T=1}^LT\sum_{d|T}^{L}\mu(d)\times d\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{T}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{T}\rfloor} iC_{iT}\times jC_{jT}\\ =&\sum_{T=1}^LT\left(\sum_{d|T}^{L}\mu(d)\times d\right)\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{T}\rfloor}iC_{iT}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{T}\rfloor}jC_{jT}\\ =&\sum_{T=1}^LT\left(\sum_{d|T}^{L}\mu(d)\times d\right)\left(\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{L}{T}\rfloor}iC_{iT}\right)^2 \end{aligned} \] 莫反的经典套路，将枚举的两项 \(i,j\) 拆出公因数 \(k,d\)，然后枚举 \(T=kd\) 并交换求和，使得枚举 \(d\) 的部分和后面 \(i,j\) 部分无关，这样就可以给和式套括号分开算了。

第一个括号预处理，筛出 \(\mu\)，枚举倍数做贡献，复杂度 \(O(L\ln L)\)。

第二个括号直接暴力算，复杂度为 \(L\times \sum_{T=1}^L\lfloor\frac{1}{T}\rfloor=O(L\ln L)\)。

总复杂度 \(O(L\ln L)\)。
